Discovery FAQs

Common questions about our professional research process.

How long does a typical research project take?

Basic packages typically take 4-6 weeks, while our Legacy Professional plan can span 3-6 months depending on the complexity and archival accessibility of your unique family history.

Do you guarantee 100% accuracy in your findings?

We adhere to the Genealogical Proof Standard (GPS). Every finding is backed by cited primary sources. If a connection cannot be proven beyond reasonable doubt, we state it clearly as a lead rather than a fact.

Can you investigate records in non-English speaking countries?

Yes. Our global team has specialists fluent in over 12 languages and expert knowledge of international archival systems in Europe, Asia, and the Americas.

What happens if no records are found?

While rare, "brick walls" do exist. In such cases, we provide a detailed 'Negative Search' report showing every archive consulted, which is invaluable for future research attempts.
