Specialized Research Pillars

We combine traditional archival prowess with modern genetic science to uncover every branch of your family tree.

Request Consultation View Packages

Lineage Probing

Tracing single surnames or complex matrilineal lines across multiple countries. We dig deep into municipal archives and censuses.

Genetic Genealogy

Using DNA matches to break through 200-year-old "brick walls." We interpret autosomal, Y-DNA, and mtDNA results for you.

Document Recovery

Professional retrieval of birth, marriage, and death certificates from global repositories. Hand-delivered or digital delivery.

Probate Research

Verifying next-of-kin for legal estates and unclaimed heritage benefits. We work with legal firms and private individuals.

Migration Histories

Mapping the voyage of your ancestors from their home ports to their final settlements. Includes historical shipping manifest data.

Narrative Writing

Turning raw data and records into a compelling, readable biography of your family. A legacy piece for future generations.

Elite Research Methodology

A rigorous phase-based approach to uncovering and verifying your history.

01

Preliminary Probe

We analyze existing family knowledge and initial DNA results to identify key archival locations and likely surnames variants.

02

Archival Deep-Dive

Our specialists visit local repositories to find primary sources like parish registers, land deeds, and court records.

03

Verification & Report

Every finding is peer-reviewed for historical accuracy before being woven into your final heritage narrative report.
